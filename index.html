WIRTGEN GROUP SUBSIDIARY BRANDS

Wirtgen Brand

The Wirtgen brand encompasses several lines of machines related to the processing of concrete. These include:

  • Cold milling machines for asphalt reclamation
  • Slipform pavers for paving streets and curbs
  • Surface miners for stripping soil

Vogele Brand

The Vogele brand consists of several models of asphalt pavers.

Hamm Brand

The Hamm brand is a line of asphalt compression machines (i.e. road rollers).

Kleemann Brand

The Kleemann brand consists of an assortment of mining equipment for crushing, sorting, and stacking rocks and minerals.
